Italien Pasta

Discription

This recepi will make the perfect main dish or it could work as an apptices for any ocation.This italian dish incldue fresh ingridients origated form italli.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolne cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut in half
  • 1 (10 ounce) can black oluves, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle italian salad dressing

Direction

  1. Step 1

    2. Gether all the ingredients

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.

  3. Step 3

    4. Combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green peppers, olives, and pimentos in a large bowl. Pour in salad dressing; toss to coat.

  4. Step 4

    5. Enjoy!
