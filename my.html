Classic Cheesecake

Discription

This is an original recipe of a cheesecake.This will be an easy to make and fast for any occation.

    Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups graham crackers crumbs
  • 1/2 cups of butter, melted (1 stick)
  • 1/3 cup of powdered sugar

    • Cheesecake

    3 (8-ounces) packages cream cheese, softened 4 eggs 1 cup of sugar 1 teaspoon of vanella 1 pint dairy sour cream (at room temperature) 1 (21-ounce) can prepared cherry, bluebarry pie filling

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°.Combine graham cracker crumbs, powdered sugar and melted butter. Press into bottom of 8-inch springform pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, eggs, sugar and vanilla until smooth. Pour mixture over prepared crust
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes (until center is set).
  4. Remove from oven and spread sour cream on top of cheesecake. Return to oven and bake an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool. Spread desired topping on cheesecake.
  6. Chill overnight. Before serving, carefully remove sides from pan. Serves 16.

Tip: minimize cracking, place shallow pan half full of hot water on lower rack of oven during baking. And be sure the sour cream is at room temperature when you spread it on.
